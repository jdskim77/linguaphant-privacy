# Linguaphant Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview
Linguaphant is a language learning flashcard app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
Linguaphant does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage
- Quiz progress and settings are stored **locally on your device**
- If you enable iCloud, your progress syncs via Apple's iCloud service. We do not have access to this data.
- Leaderboard participation uses Apple's CloudKit with an anonymous device identifier. No personal information is stored.

## Third-Party Services
Linguaphant does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## In-App Purchases
Tip jar purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy
Linguaphant does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
